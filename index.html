Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how LaScheda handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

LaScheda collects personal data if you explicitly give consent through an in-app consent form.

If you agree, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Location information (country, city, and postal code)
  • Health or user-provided data (such as body weight or other information you voluntarily enter in the app)
  • App usage data (such as screens visited, interactions, and session duration)
  • Device and technical information (such as device model, operating system version, and app version)

This data is collected only after you provide explicit consent and can be disabled at any time (if the feature is available in the app).

This data is transmitted to Mixpanel for analytics purposes.

For more information, please refer to Mixpanel’s Privacy Policy: https://mixpanel.com/legal/privacy-policy/

2. How We Use Information

Any access to photos or camera is used only to allow you to select or upload images. We do not analyze, upload, or store this data on external servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

If you don't provide consent all you data are stored in your device only.

4. Data Security

We prioritize protecting your data. Any processing of media happens locally on your device unless explicitly triggered by you.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
