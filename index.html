Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how LaScheda handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data We Collect

LaScheda collects data if you explicitly give consent through an in-app dialog form.

If you agree, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Location information: (country, city, postal code and IP)
  • User-provided data (such as body weight or other information you voluntarily enter in the app)
  • App usage data (such as screens visited, interactions, and session duration)
  • Device and technical information (such as device model, operating system version, and app version)

This data is collected only after you provide explicit consent and can be disabled at any time (if the feature is available in the app) and is transmitted to Mixpanel for analytics purposes.

For more information, please refer to Mixpanel’s Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Information

Any access to photos or camera is used only to allow you to select or upload images. We do not analyze, upload, or store this data on external servers.

Collected data (as mentioned in section 1. such as App usage) are used for:

  • Improve App functionality and performance.
  • Understand how the App is used.
  • Decide what new features to implement or improve.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

If you don't provide consent all you data are stored in your device only.

4. Data Security

We prioritize protecting your data. Any processing of media happens locally on your device unless explicitly triggered by you.

5. Children's Privacy

LaScheda does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
